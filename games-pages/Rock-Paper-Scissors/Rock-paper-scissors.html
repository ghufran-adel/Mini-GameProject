Rock, Paper, Scissors: is a simple hand game played between two people. Each player simultaneously forms one of three shapes with their hand: Rock (a fist), Paper (an open hand), or Scissors (a V-shape). The game's outcomes are determined by rules where Rock beats Scissors, Scissors beat Paper, and Paper beats Rock. It's often used as a quick and fun decision-making tool or a game to settle disputes.
