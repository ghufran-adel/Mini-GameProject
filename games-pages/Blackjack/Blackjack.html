A simplified version of Blackjack.
The computer will pick a number between 1 and 5.
The user will pick a number between 1 and 5.
The computer will add the user’s number to it’s number.
It will check if the total value is 21.
This will repeat until the total is 21 or above.
The goal for the user is to try to get 21 exactly!
If their total is above 21, they lose.
